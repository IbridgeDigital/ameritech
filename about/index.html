About us

We have been providing IT services to small and mid-sized businesses for more than a decade. Our dedicated team of specialists include resources with deep technical skills and know how, some with Fortune 500 background. In addition to servicing IT networks and business applications, our dedicated team of professionals include website and digital marketing experts, and an impressive portfolio, some of which has been showcased on our website.

Although we can service any industry, we have carved our niche servicing a handful of verticals, with healthcare and legal being on top of the list. We have recently added hospitality domain to our portfolio.

There are many reasons to engage Ameritech for Managed Services, the foremost being that you can stop worrying about a problem or project that is diverting your attention from your business stability and growth. We provide end-to-end solutions in sales, service, installation and support that are fully customized to meet your business needs.

In addition, our team of digital experts can help get more customers to you, potentially increasing your revenue by providing a solid ROI for your marketing dollars. We offer a strategy, and we partner with you to manage your online marketing spend, by combining SEO and online advertising with conversion optimization, to improve likelihood of visitors become paying clients.
