LOCATION

JUST MINUTES TO ANYWHERE IN THE WORLD.

It’s hard to imagine a better location for a world-class community than ours. Peña Station NEXT is situated at the global midpoint between Tokyo and Frankfurt, allowing companies to conduct business across multiple time zones at once and conveniently travel to Europe and Asia directly. Located at the front door of Denver International Airport (DEN), Peña Station NEXT is only 12 minutes by train to the fifth-busiest airport in the U.S.—serving more than 200 destinations nonstop, including 25 direct international routes. Regionally, Peña Station NEXT is 28 minutes to downtown Denver, 26 minutes to the Denver Tech Center and 40 minutes to Boulder. When you’re at Peña Station NEXT, you’re just minutes to anywhere in the world.
